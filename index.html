The Lamborghini Aventador SVJ represents the pinnacle of Lamborghini's engineering excellence. With its aerodynamically optimized design and powerful V12 engine, this limited edition supercar delivers an unparalleled driving experience. The Ferrari 488 Pista is the track-focused evolution of the 488 GTB, incorporating technology derived directly from Ferrari's Formula 1 experience. "Pista" means "track" in Italian, and this car lives up to its name with exceptional performance and handling characteristics.

With a twin-turbocharged V8 engine that won the International Engine of the Year award four years running, the 488 Pista delivers breathtaking acceleration and a spine-tingling exhaust note. Its aerodynamic enhancements provide increased downforce while maintaining the stunning beauty that defines Ferrari's design language.
